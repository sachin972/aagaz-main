About AAGAZ

AAGAZ, an initiative by HBTU students,is a one-of-a-kind sports festival aimed at making sports professional at college level. AAGAZ, as the name suggests, revolves around one word – begining.

It is this very passion for sport that drives everyone at AAGAZ to make the dream of a professionally run sporting league at a collegiate level a reality for all fans and players alike. AAGAZ is the perfect antidote for those who have sport in their DNA and they want to proove themselves.
